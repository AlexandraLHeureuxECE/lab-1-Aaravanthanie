How to Play

Objective: Be the first player to get three of your marks in a row (horizontally, vertically, or diagonally).

Controls

Mouse:

  • Click on any empty cell to place your mark ('X' or 'O').

Keyboard:

  • Press Tab to focus on the game board. The top-left cell will be selected.
  • Use the Arrow Keys (↑, ↓, ←, →) to navigate between cells.
  • Press Enter on a selected empty cell to place your mark.
